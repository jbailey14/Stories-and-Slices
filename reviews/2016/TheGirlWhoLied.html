12/14/2016 - The Girl who Lied

Here is the review on the second book I finished this week: The Girl who Lied.

This book was slightly predictable but very well written. I finished this in only a couple days because I couldn't stop turning the pages. It is about a woman who left her small Irish village a decade previous to hide a secret. Now her father has fallen ill and she must return home. She thinks her secret is safe, but others know about it and aren't willing to let it alone.

I recommend this book to anyone who likes psychological thrillers or a quick easy read.
