6/6/2016 - The Butterfly Garden

DAMN! I just finished a book that I don't know how to review. I read it in a couple days (which is amazing for me). But it's the most macabre piece of work if I've read in a LLLONNNNGGGG time.

If you've got a thick skin and like an all consuming book...The Butterfly Garden was captivating. I was always curious to see what was happening on the next page and there was a GREAT twist at the end.

Note: PLEASE read the summary first as I am very serious...the book IS NOT for everyone.
