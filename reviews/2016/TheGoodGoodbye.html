8/17/2016 - The Good Goodbye

Finished a book a while ago that I don't believe I reviewed. I finished The Good Goodbye.

There is a fire at a college campus. Afterward, one is dead, and two are in ICU. How did this happen? Was it an accident or revenge? And how do the families deal with this tragedy? Read it to find out!!

I recommend this book to people who like a good mystery. There were a few surprises even for me!
