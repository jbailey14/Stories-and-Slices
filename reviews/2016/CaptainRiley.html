2/8/2016 - Captain Riley

Just finished another book. Captain Riley by Fernando Gamboa is a GREAT action adventure book. It's set in the 30's where a band of smugglers get caught up in a super secret plot on how the Nazis will win the war. There are twists and turns up until the very end. I always like a book that verges down a path I wasn't expecting...and this did that very well. I highly suggest it for ANY fan of thrillers or action/adventures.

Malia's Pizza Rating

A heart pounding 5 stars!
