12/21/2016 - John Dies at the End

Time for another book review!

I just finished a book I would put in my top 5 favorite books read in 2016. It's called John Dies at the End and it is SUPER addictive!

It's about two men who take an other-worldly drug and end up able to see the dark forces in our world that come from a parallel universe. What I love about this book is these two young men aren't strong and brilliant, they simply do their best to maneuver and survive these evil spirits and beasts that are after them. It is full of comedy and page turning action.

I recommend this book to science fiction lovers, readers who like dark stories, and everyone in between...this was a seriously fun read.
