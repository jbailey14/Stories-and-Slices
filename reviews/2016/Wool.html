2/22/2016 - Wool

Finished reading Wool by Hugh Howey this weekend. I FLEW through this book. Couldn't put it down. It's a dystopian book where everyone is left on a toxic earth live down deep in a silo. The plot twists and turns, but without giving too much away, I'll say that a few citizens find out their being lied to...and want to change the way things run in the silo. People I recommend this book to: EVERYONE...Seriously..if you can read you should go pick up this book...now....what are you waiting for?
