7/14/2016 - Airframe

Just finished the book AIRFRAME by Michael Crichton. I picked this book because it checked off a box on my 2016 reading challenge list. It was "The first book you see in a bookstore."

Once again Crichton does a great job of writing a page turner. I am not a fast reader, and I had 1/2 done in the first day I picked it up! I would recommend this book to anyone looking for a good summer read!
