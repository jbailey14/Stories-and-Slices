2/29/2016 - Blue Monday

I've gotten really lucky lately with books. I finished Blue Monday last week. It's a great read about a kidnapper and the therapist who unknowingly gets thrown into the investigation to find them. I would recommend this book to someone who likes a good who-done-it novel.

Malia's Pizza Rating

8 finger bitten nails.
