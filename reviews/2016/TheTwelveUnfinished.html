12/14/2016 - The Twelve: book 2 in The Passage Triology

I finished two books in the last week...here is the first review of The Twelve, which is the second book in The Passage trilogy.

This book covers the days right after the infected break from their compound and how many people fight to survive. It also advances to the main storyline with the group who are trying to find the twelve original vampires destroy them.

There is only one book left...and I can't wait to see how Justin Cronin wraps everything up. Again, I recommend this series to those who love horror and to those who like all things vampire.
