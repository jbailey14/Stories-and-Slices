8/28/2016 - Rage

Just finished Rage by Zygmunt Miloszewski. It fit the "translated into English" box on my 2016 reading challenge.

I knew I would like this book as it's a murder mystery. It's about a prosecutor who finds a skeleton that looks decades old. Turns out it's only weeks old...AND made of multiple donors. I will admit it took a little while to get used to the flow of writing (as do most translated books)...but the story line was great and there was a wonderful twist in the end. I think anyone who likes the murder mystery genre would enjoy this read.

Malia's Pizza Rating

A half and half pizza...chef's choice...full of surprises.
