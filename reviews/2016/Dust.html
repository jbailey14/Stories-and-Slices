5/23/2016 - Dust

I just finished the last book in the Wool Omnibus Trilogy. Dust - by Hugh Howey is the perfect finish to the two previous books. I can't describe how amazing this story is without giving things away.

I recommend these books to ANYONE who likes to read. They are mesmerizing and thought provoking. I wish soooooo badly that I could choose them for my book club, but we have a 300 page limit.
