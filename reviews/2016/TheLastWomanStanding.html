10/29/2016 - The Last Woman Standing

Finished a book last night...and it wasn't a murder mystery!!!

The Last Woman Standing is a historical fiction about one woman who influenced the stand off at the O.K. Corral. I would say this is technically a romance novel...but not in the Nora Roberts kind of way. It is well written, sexy and still full of wild west gun fights over land and women. It is the first novel I have sat and been consumed with in awhile. I would recommend it to anyone who would like to see the fairly accurate story of what led to the gunfight at the corral, or to someone who enjoys romantic novels.
