8/3/2016 - The Last Girl

Finished another book yesterday. It's part of another dystopian trilogy called The Last Girl.

It's about a time in the future when the birth rate of females is close to 0%. There is a girl who is kept with other young women and they are treated like captives. She finally says enough is enough and makes a break for it.

I REALLY liked this novel...and I already have the 2nd book on pre-order when it comes out in late September. I highly recommend it to anyone who loves the Hunger Games, Divergent, or Maze Runner series.
