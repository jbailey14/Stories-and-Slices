10/7/2016 - Blood on the Tracks

Ok..here we go...another murder mystery review.

I finished Blood on the tracks. It's about a former Marine turned railroad cop with her K-9 partner. There is a homicide linked to the hobos who catch the trains that she gets pulled into to solve.

It's a pretty good book. It had me wanting to find out more, and I'm excited to read the next book in the series when it comes out. I wouldn't say it was amazing...or a must read...but it was good.

I recommend this book only to those who are diehard murder mystery fans. Otherwise there are better books that I've reviewed if you're just reaching into this genre. And I know this is the type of book I read a lot...so next up I'm pulling out a classic. #tryingtobediverse

Malia's Pizza Rating

Sausage. Nothing out of this world...but still good.
