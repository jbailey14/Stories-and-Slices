5/10/2016 - No Coming Back

Just finished a book called "No Coming Back." It was a middle of the road kind of murder mystery...there were many times I felt the writing was stained. The end however, was one of the best twists I've read in awhile. I would probably only recommend this book to readers who love murder mysteries and can't get enough of them. Otherwise, save your eyes for some of the greats.
