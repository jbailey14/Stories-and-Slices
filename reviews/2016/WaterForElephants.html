3/24/2016 - Water for Elephants

Just finished Water for Elephants. WOW! I didn't really know what to expect. I had recognized the title since it was very popular when it first came out. But beyond that it was a mystery.

This book is so captivating. It follows a young man who joins the circus in the 30's and his adventures in animals, love, and surviving! Very well written. I felt like I was just getting started with the story line when it was over. I wished it would have gone on a lot longer.

I highly recommend this to all book lovers. And it would be an incredible choice for a book club!
