10/8/2016 - Of Mice and Men

I promised everyone a classic...here it is.

Of Mice and Men is easy to read and quite short (like 116 pages). It is of two men traveling for work during the Great Depression. One is large, strong, and has the mind of a five year old. The other takes care of him even when he causes trouble. Their only dream is to get enough money to own their own piece of land.

I recommend this one to everyone. First, it's a classic and influenced many other forms of art. Second, if you're going to try a classic and it's not really your thing....this one is nice and short.
