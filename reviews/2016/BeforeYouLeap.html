10/21/2016 - Before You Leap

Just finished another book called Before You Leap. It is about a man whose twin sister was killed 10 years ago. Now, the man convicted of the crime has been deemed innocent and released from prison....and he's on his way to confront the brother. Things spiral out of control and the protagonist finds himself in a situation he doesn't know how to get out of.

This was an alright read...I gave it 3 out of 5 stars. Not the most dripping tale, but worth the read if you fly through books. Otherwise I'd say there are better out there.
