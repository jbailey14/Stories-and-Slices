6/16/2016 - Blood Defense

Just finished a murder mystery called Blood Defense. I speed through it, as the writing is very smooth. I wouldn't say it was anything out of this world, but the twist in the Epilogue was STELLAR!

I would recommend this book for anyone who likes the murder mystery genre OR is looking for a quick read that is entertaining to the end.
