1/3/2017 - Unbroken

Time for the first book review of 2017! I finished Unbroken.

It's a great book if you can get through a story of shit being rained down upon someone repeatedly for multiple years. This is the true story of an American man in World War II who goes through the worst trails imaginable. I want so badly to give more detail, but I think it was much more captivating because I didn't know what was going to happen to him next. I will at least say that it does have WAY too many mentions of sharks for my liking!

I would recommend this book to those who love war stories, for those who love biographies, and to those who love history. One warning, this book is not for the faint of heart.
