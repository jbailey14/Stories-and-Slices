2/9/2017 - The Color of Heaven

Book review time: I finished The Color of Heaven yesterday. It's a contemporary fiction about a woman who is having more than her share of tragedy, and to top it all off, she almost dies. There is a MAJOR twist towards the end...but without giving it away this story is about moving past hard times, forgiving, making amends, and looking forward to the beauty the future holds.

I would recommend this book to the people who like Hallmark movies or to people who like stories about self growth.

Malia's Pizza Rating

A slice of heaven *wink*
