5/22/2015 - Grimm’s Fairy Tales

I didn't think it would happen! I have my first negative review of a book. Grimm's Fairy Tales just doesn't do anything for me. I found the stories to end so oddly that I couldn't even finish the collection. There was no morale to the story, no climax and no resolution. I know that a lot of these were oral and or written long ago when things were done differently, but there seemed to be no point. People may as well have read from the farmer's almanac to put their kids to sleep. Sorry if anyone liked it...I just couldn't.
