4/28/2015 - The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes

I finished The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes today. It is a good and quick read. I love the stories but honestly love what the BBC and Benedict Cumberbatch have done with the character more. The man in the book just seems too normal to be as observant as he is.

If you're an avid reader I would say pick this book up and have some fun reading it. If you don't consume books like they're going out of style I say pick up the BBC TV series...it's GREAT!

Malia's Pizza Rating

One magnifying glass, a trench coat and adventures that are afoot.
