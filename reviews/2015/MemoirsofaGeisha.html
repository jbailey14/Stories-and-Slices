8/26/2015 - Memoirs of a Geisha

Finished Memoirs of a Geisha last week. What a great story! I'm having a hard time finding the right words to review it though. I love the Japanese culture and their literature. It isn't a story for those who need "ride off into the sunset" types of endings. It's well written and intriguing. It brings light to a world that seems to be from the imagination but was so real and intense. Highly recommended to those who are interested in a culture that is very different from our own.
