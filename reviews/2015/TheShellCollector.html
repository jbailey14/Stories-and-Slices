11/2/2015 - The Shell Collector

Just finished reading The Shell Collector by Anthony Doerr. It is a collection of short stories. I wasn't in love with the writing style at first...it took me a while to get the cadence of the author's voice. I also think I liked this book more because I was able to break down all the questions, and symbolism and opinions I had with my book club. The stories are often about the juxtaposition between nature and city life. The characters are interesting and each an adventurer in their own way.

I think this is a book better suited for avid readers who like to interpret stories and infer their own deeper meaning. If you like that...then this collection is up your alley.

Malia's Pizza Rating

4 out of 5 stars
