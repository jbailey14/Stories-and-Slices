10/8/2015 - The Fault in Our Stars

Finished a book last week for book club. The Fault in Our Stars was, to be honest, not a book I cared to pick up. I figured the story had been told multiple times. Kid gets cancer, falls in love and dies anyways.

This was soooo much more then that. I liked the dark humor that permeated the story line. It was an easy read and a true page turner. I didn't expect to like this book as much as I did. I recommend it to everyone as a book to make you laugh, cry and end with a renewed positive outlook on life and love.

Malia's Pizza Rating

4/5 pizza slices.
