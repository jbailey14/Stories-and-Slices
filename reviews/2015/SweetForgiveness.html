7/9/2015 - Sweet Forgiveness

Just finished a GREAT book. This one has been my favorite of the summer so far. Sweet Forgiveness is a book about reaching out to those you've hurt in the past, asking for forgiveness and shedding the guilt from your life. The sappy romance wasn't so bad either ;) This one is a quick read and I think everyone could take something from it!

Malia's Pizza Rating

A large box of self meditation and growth wrapped up with a love story bow on top!
