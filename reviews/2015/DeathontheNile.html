9/11/2015 - Death on the Nile

Just finished Death on the Nile by Agatha Christie for our book club. What a GREAT murder mystery. She keeps you in suspense throughout and there are some fantastic twists and turns as you go. It's a fun, quick read from one of the most widely published author of all time.
