11/19/2015 - A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms

I finished A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms yesterday. I would have liked the book from the start a lot more if I had known it WASN'T book 6 of the Fire and Ice series. Instead, it is a collection of short stories woven together that is set 100 years before the main story line. After finding that out (by Googling) I really enjoyed the stories as a separate piece. I would recommend this book to anyone who is already reading the Game of Thrones series.

Malia's Pizza Rating

4/5 stars
