2/16/2015 - Crime and Punishment

I finally finished this Russian classic. Overall, I find some of the themes quite interesting such as the degradation into delirium and paranoia after committing a violent crime. What made this a difficult read for me, was the lollygagging side stories that droned on forever and could have been cut down considerably. It was so bad that I often forgot the last place the main storyline left off.

Malia's Pizza Rating

Meh.
