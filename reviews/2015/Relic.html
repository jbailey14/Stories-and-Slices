8/5/2015 - Rellic

Finished reading Relic earlier this week (yes, the novel that the 90's movie was based on). This story was a first time collaboration between two different authors..and you can tell. I love a good Sci-Fi page turner. This one has a lot of dry delivery of information in the first half...and then really picks up into a good book in the second half. The biggest redeeming quality, for me, was the HUGE twist in the epilogue. I still like the writings of James Rollins a whole lot more. I would only recommend this book if you are a Sci-Fi lover.
